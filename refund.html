Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

Success-Based Service

Our service operates on a performance basis. If we do not secure savings on your submitted bills, you will not be charged for our negotiation service.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be granted if:

  • An incorrect charge was made.
  • A duplicate payment occurred.
  • A billing error is verified by our support team.

Non-Refundable Fees

Fees are generally non-refundable once savings have been successfully obtained and reported to the customer.

Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, contact our support team within 30 days of the charge and provide:

  • Account information
  • Transaction details
  • Reason for the request

Refund requests will be reviewed within 5-10 business days.
